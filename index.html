About Me

My favorite hobby is solving my Rubik's Cubes. I can solve 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, skewb, pyraminx, megaminx, gearcube, and ghost-cubes. I am 11 years old and I am in 6th grade. I started cubing 1 1/2 years ago when I was ten years old. Cubing requires dedication, focus, problem solving, and intuition, especially for big cubes such as the 4x4 and megaminx
